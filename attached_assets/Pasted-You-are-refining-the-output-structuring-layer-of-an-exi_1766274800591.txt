You are refining the **output structuring layer** of an existing Python FastAPI backend for a legal conveyancing AI system.

Context:

* Local Authority Search extraction is already working
* Findings are accurate but presented flatly
* Deterministic risk rules already exist
* This task improves **structure, prioritisation, and solicitor readability only**

Objective:
Improve the **presentation and structuring** of Local Authority Search results so that:

* Findings are grouped logically
* High-signal issues are clearly distinguished
* Output is easier for solicitors to scan and prioritise
* No legal advice or new interpretation is introduced

STRICT SCOPE LIMITS:

* Do NOT change OCR
* Do NOT change extraction logic
* Do NOT change Azure OpenAI prompts
* Do NOT change risk rules or severity logic
* Do NOT add new findings
* Modify output formatting / structuring only

---

### Structural improvements to implement

1️⃣ **Group Local Land Charges by type**

Instead of a single flat list, structure Local Land Charges as:

* Section 106 Agreements
* Tree Preservation Orders
* Smoke Control Orders
* Other Charges (if any)

Each entry should remain factual and unchanged.

---

2️⃣ **Improve Planning Register entries**

For each Planning Register entry, present fields explicitly:

* Reference
* Description
* Decision status (if known)
* Note if the entry is historic or context-only

Do NOT infer compliance or relevance.

---

3️⃣ **Clarify Road Adoption findings**

For Road Adoption issues:

* State the factual finding
* Add a neutral implication label such as:

  * "Private maintenance obligations may apply"
* Do NOT recommend actions

---

4️⃣ **Clarify CIL output**

For CIL:

* Clearly distinguish between:

  * Presence of a CIL regime
  * Actual liability (if stated)
* Use wording such as:

  * "CIL regime identified; liability depends on development history"

---

5️⃣ **Add a lightweight “Key Issues Identified” summary**

At the top of the output, add a short summary block:

Example:

* Section 106 agreement affecting the property
* Road serving the property is not publicly adopted
* Tree Preservation Order applies
* No enforcement notices or planning breaches identified

Rules:

* Summary must only reference extracted findings
* No severity scoring
* No recommendations
* No new interpretation

---

6️⃣ **Optional: Add neutral issue labels (non-severity)**

Where helpful, tag findings with neutral labels such as:

* "Transaction constraint"
* "Ongoing obligation"
* "Use / alteration restriction"
* "Environmental regulation"

Do NOT use High / Medium / Low severity labels here.

---

### Output expectations

The final output should:

* Remain fully deterministic
* Be auditable and explainable
* Match solicitor expectations
* Be suitable for direct UI rendering
* Improve scanability without adding risk

Deliverable:
An updated Local Authority Search output structure that presents findings in a clearer, prioritised, and solicitor-friendly format without changing the underlying extraction or risk logic.
